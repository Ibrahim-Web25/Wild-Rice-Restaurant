The Experience

A ballroom of flavour, crafted in silence and served with grace.

Set on the lobby level of the new wing at Serena Hotel, Wild Rice brings together the cuisines of Southeast Asia under one hushed, timbered roof. Live stations, picturesque views and a warm golden glow make every visit an unforgettable affair.

From the fragrant Tom Yum Goong to the charcoal-kissed Ikan Bakar, each plate is a study in definition, quality and efficiency — the marks of a house that takes its craft seriously.

— The House of Wild Rice
